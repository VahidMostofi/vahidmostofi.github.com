This is a tutorial for a work-in-progress application called “with audio”.

This application allows you to listen to different blog posts instead of having to read them.

Notes for the tutorial:

  • You are using the free tier, which gives you 2 credits for now. You will receive more credits daily. Each credit can be used to generate audio for one Item.
  • This application also functions as an RSS management tool. Use the Feeds tab to find and follow existing or new RSS feeds. Items from the RSS feeds you follow will appear in your Home page.
  • You can bookmark or like Items and view them in My Items tab.
  • Items you have requested to get the audio format can be found in the Player page with ready-to-listen audio.
  • And finally, you can add custom links by pasting a URL in My Items page.
